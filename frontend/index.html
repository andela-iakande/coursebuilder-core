The challenge for African journalists

Journalists all over the world face the same issue. Our audiences are changing, and expect modern storytelling in digital formats. We must learn to tell their stories in formats that work on computer monitors and on phones, while at the same time developing the skills to interrogate online information and sources of data honestly and with integrity.

But our newsrooms are under-resourced, and don’t have the time and money to invest in high quality training to meet these new demands. In Africa, these problems compounded with the issues of low levels of internet access and smartphone adoption, and a chronic shortage of digital skills.

That’s why Code for Africa, in partnership with Google News Lab and The World Bank, has created the StoryLab Academy.

Welcome to the first Massively Open Online Course (MOOC) designed specifically for African journalists. Through the StoryLab Academy we aim to help develop world-class storytellers and investigators at the cutting edge of their craft.

How It Works

Set your goals:

Tailor courses to your most pressing needs. Create a learning plan that’s right for you whether you want to gain new skills on multimedia or investigative journalism or learn how to access new digital revenue streams.

Learn from experience:

Watch video tutorials from the world’s best digital journalism experts. Much of the instructional material was developed within Africa. The hands-on data journalism training materials which complement Google’s online tutorials were developed and tested in the Sudan and Kenya by the World Bank.

Apply your knowledge:

Test what you have learned and get practical easy to use tips and resources for your next digital journalism projects.

Track your progress:

Your personal dashboard will help keep you motivated and on track with your learning plan.

Keep Going:

Need help fast while on a deadline? Tailor courses to your most pressing needs and access video explainers, concise tip-sheets and exercises to immediately use the needed tool in minutes.
